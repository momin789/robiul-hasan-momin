About Me

I'm Robiul Hasan Momin, a creative and detail-oriented Graphic Designer with over 3 years of experience delivering compelling visual content across print and digital platforms. I specialize in branding, social media graphics, and promotional design that enhances engagement and strengthens brand identity.

I have a proven track record of collaborating with editorial, marketing, and creative teams to produce high-quality static and motion assets. My design work has directly contributed to increased performance and visibility for multiple brands. With a strong command of tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, and Figma, I combine technical skills with creative thinking to solve design challenges efficiently.

I hold a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design from the BASIS Institute of Technology & Management (BITM). I’m passionate about visual storytelling and always committed to delivering impactful design solutions.
